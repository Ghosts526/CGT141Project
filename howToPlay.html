Controls

  • W - Moves your ship up
  • S - Moves your ship down
  • Space - Fires your primary weapon (Blaster)
  • E - Fires your secondary weapon (Missile)
  • Left Click - Interact with buttons

Objectives

  • Survive as long as possible while defeating waves of enemies and by upgrading your ship.
  • Each wave becomes progressively harder, introducing tougher enemies and faster spawn rates.

Upgrades & Progression

From the main menu, you can enhance your ship with upgrades such as:

  • Increase Health
  • Increade Firerate
  • Decrease Missile Cooldown
  • Increase Shield Health
  • Decrease Shield Cooldown

Upgrades persist until data has been cleared, either by the user or by the browser. These upgrades are use to help you survive more intense waves of enemies.
